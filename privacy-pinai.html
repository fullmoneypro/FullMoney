Privacy & Security Commitment of Pinai App

  • 100% Free of Personal Data: We respect your privacy. This app does not ask for your email, phone number, or real name.
  • Private Conversations: AI chats are not stored on our servers. Every time the app is opened, the conversation history is automatically cleared.
  • Local Storage: Data from the Note feature is strictly stored only within your device's local memory.
  • Cloud Quota System & Anonymous Account: Quota balances are solely for accessing app features. Your Quota is safely stored on our cloud servers using a randomized Device ID.
  • Quota Vault Feature: To prevent losing your quota when changing devices or clearing data, you can create a Password in the 'Account' menu. With your Pinai ID and Password, your quota can be restored at any time.
  • Account & Data Deletion: You have the full right to request the deletion of your account and data (including your quota balance) from our servers. Since your account is anonymous, you can submit a deletion request by sending an email to fullmoneypro@gmail.com along with your Pinai ID. We will permanently process the data deletion within a maximum of 24 hours.
  • Advertising Services (AdMob): We use Google AdMob to provide free quota features. This system uses your device's standard Advertising ID anonymously.
  • Third-Party Services & Browsing Privacy: The Pinai app operates using web browser technology (based on Google WebView/Chrome) to display services and content from third parties. We do not collect, store, or manage your activity data outside the Pinai system, and we are absolutely not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or security of those third-party services. All your browsing activities are fully subject to the rules of the platforms you visit. We strongly advise you to read the relevant Third-Party Privacy Policies, as well as the Google Privacy Policy, considering the use of browser technology in this app. By using Pinai, you consciously agree to these terms and release us from any liability or legal claims related to third parties.
